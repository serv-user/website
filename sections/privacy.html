Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and handle personal data responsibly. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Data Collection

Information submitted through this website (via our contact forms) is collected only for the purposes of:

  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Processing job applications
  • Providing staffing services
  • Communication about our services

Form Submissions

Contact forms use your email client to send messages directly. When you submit a form, your default email application opens with the message pre-filled. No data is stored on our servers — it goes directly to your email client.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties. Form submissions go directly from your email client to info@raduservices.dk.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • File a complaint with Danish data authorities
